When you stay at Johnny's country house, you'll be on the riverwalk, within a 10-minute drive of Banff Gondola and Banff Centre for Arts and Creativity. Nestled within majestic peaks of Banff National Park. Johnny's country house is your luxury base-camp for both your summer and winter adventures. In the summer, explore the high alpine meadows as you take in uninterrupted mountain views. Come winter, treat yourself to effortless access to the slopes of village as you wake up to Canada's best snow.
